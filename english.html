About Me

I am a graduate of International Relations BA degree from the "Pontificia Universidad Católica del Ecuador". Also, I hold a MA in Philosophy, Politics, and Economics (PPE) from the "Universidad de las Américas". Currently, I'm studyng a MA in Education & a MSc in Artificial Intelligence. I work in the intersection between Philosophy & Computer Science. My main research focus is Philosophy of AI. As secondary interests I have: Political Philosophy, Philosophy of Mind, Comparative Philosophy (West-China), Logics, and Ethics. Beside my research profile, I am also a fiction writer & a game developer, where I have a focus on technologies such as Python & C++. My goal in dev world is to become a developer who dedicates his time to work in NPC AI. I also do small projects in AI, general software dev & web dev (Currently I'm learning CSS & JS).
