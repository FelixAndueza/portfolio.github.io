About Me

I am a graduate of International Relations BA degree from the "Pontificia Universidad Católica del Ecuador". Currently, I am a student of a MA degree in Philosophy, Politics, and Economics (PPE) from the "Universidad de las Américas". Currently, I am also studying a Computer Science Path in Codecademy. I consider myself a proactive and responsible person who seeks new challenges for my development. My main focus is AI ethics. My secondary focus are Existencialism & IR Constructivism. My interests are Political Theory, Behavioural Economics, Philosophical Anthropology, Chinese Philosophy, & Philosophy of Mind. Beside my research profile, I am also a fiction writer & a game developer, where I have a focus on technologies such as Python & C++. My goal in dev world is to become a developer who dedicates his time to work in NPC AI. I also do small projects in AI, general software dev & web dev (Currently I'm learning CSS & JS).
